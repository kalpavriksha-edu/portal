About Kalpavriksha

An initiative to bridge the gap in college education and what is needed by industry.

Kalpavriksha was created with a vision to make every engineering student industry ready through practical learning, mentorship and real world experience.

Our Mission

To empower students with the right skills, engineering mindset and industry exposure to build successful careers.

Develop an ecosystem to nurture engineers from every college in India to become World Class Software Engineers.

Students currently in 3rd or 4th year of any branch are welcome to join this program. This program is designed for students who are committed to build their career in Software Engineering.
